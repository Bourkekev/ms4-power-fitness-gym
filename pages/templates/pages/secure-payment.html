MEANS OF PAYMENT

At Power Fitness, we accept credit/debit cards such as Mastercard and Visa

YOU CAN PURCHASE AT POWER FITNESS WITH PEACE OF MIND

We use Stripe as our payment gateway. Stripe handles all the secure credit card transactions, and none of your financial details are saved on our website. Millions of businesses of all sizes—from startups to large enterprises—use Stripe’s software and APIs to accept payments, send payouts, and manage their businesses online.

ABOUT STRIPE

Anyone involved with the processing, transmission, or storage of card data must comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS). Stripe has been audited by an independent PCI Qualified Security Assessor (QSA) and is certified as a PCI Level 1 Service Provider. This is the most stringent level of certification available in the payments industry.
